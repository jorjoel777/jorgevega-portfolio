Who AM I

I'm Jorge Vega Brenes, a 48-year-old Full Stack Developer
based in Spain. Since 2019, I’ve been working as a freelance
developer, combining technical precision with creative problem-
solving. My background includes developing both frontend and
backend solutions using popular frameworks like React and Vue,
as well as integrating content-driven experiences through
modern CMS platforms. I thrive in crafting functional, elegant
interfaces and resilient backend systems that not only meet the
brief—but elevate it. Passionate about innovation, I believe in
delivering code that communicates, connects, and grows with
people.
